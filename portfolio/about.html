I'm a passionate second-year B.Tech Computer Science student at Adwaita Mission Institute of Technology (A.M.I.T Shivdham). When I'm not coding, I'm usually exploring new frameworks, reading about product design, or debugging something at 1am with a very large cup of coffee. ☕
